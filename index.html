Welcome to Horizons Engineering

Horizons Engineering, Inc. provides a wide range of civil and environmental engineering and land surveying services to clients throughout northern New England. The diversity of experience of the professionals at Horizons ensures a comprehensive approach to problem solving and value added service. We are qualified to handle the management of large, diverse projects. If a project requires a specialized expertise or aspect of study, their extensive relationships with other specialists and professionals can fill any needs.

Contact Us Today!

Services We Provide

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering, design and permitting require an integrated approach to solving the complex requirements of local, state, and federal regulations. Horizons Engineering’s expertise and familiarity with the technical requirements and understanding of the approval process, while effectively utilizing cutting edge computer technology, is critical to a project’s schedule, budget, and ultimate success.
Learn More

Environmental

Horizons Engineering understands the environmental compliance challenges facing lending institutions, facility owners/operators, and property developers in today’s highly regulated world. The professionals at Horizons Engineering work diligently to protect clients from unnecessary exposure to environmental liability, while remaining very aware of the importance of keeping assessment-related costs down and turn around times to a minimum.
Learn More

Land Surveying

Horizons Engineering is a full service land surveying firm serving northern New England. They have a diverse set of skills and a comprehensive array of surveying tools at their disposal to meet the needs of a wide range of projects. With a strong commitment to their clients and to the land they tread upon, Horizons approaches each project with an emphasis on public service, efficiency and professionalism.
